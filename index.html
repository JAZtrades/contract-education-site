Who This Is For

Crypto is confusing when nobody explains it simply.

If you are curious about cryptocurrency but do not want to learn from random videos, hype, or complicated online threads, I can help you slow it down and understand the basics safely.

The goal is not to overwhelm you with every technical detail. The goal is to help you understand what you are looking at, what the risks are, and what questions you should be asking before using crypto tools.
