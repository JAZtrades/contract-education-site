Safety Boundaries

Education only. You stay in control.

John will not ask for passwords, seed phrases, private keys, authentication codes, remote wallet control, or custody of funds. You remain responsible for all accounts, security choices, and transactions.

No custody

I do not take possession of money, crypto, wallets, accounts, or devices.

No guarantees

I do not promise profits, recovery of funds, or specific financial outcomes.

No investment advice

I explain concepts and publicly available information, not what you should buy or sell.

No secrets shared

Never send passwords, private keys, recovery phrases, or authentication codes through the form.
