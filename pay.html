Recommended process

How a paid session gets confirmed

  1. Free intro call.The client chooses a current available time through the secure Proton booking page.
  2. Scope confirmed.John confirms the service, price, format, timing, payment schedule, and education-only boundaries.
  3. Agreement signed and verified.The client-specific scope agreement must be completed and independently verified before payment access can be issued.
  4. Private payment access.The client receives a one-time, expiring way to open only the exact service, installment, and price John authorized.
  5. Stripe-hosted checkout.Payment details are entered only on Stripe’s secure website. Returning to this site does not by itself prove payment.
  6. Paid appointment access.The paid-session booking step is released only after the backend verifies the required payment.
